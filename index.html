The problem

Livestock theft costs the global farming sector billions annually. In South Africa alone: R1.6 billion per year. 2.7 million animals stolen in the last recorded period. Existing solutions are failing.

Wired Minds builds real-time GPS tracking, AI anomaly detection, and automated alerts that give farmers immediate visibility and response capability — at a price point that works for real farms.
